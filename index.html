RULES

This is a dice rolling game whereby two players compete on reaching 100 points.
The first to reach 100 points wins the game:-
  • If one rolls the dice and gets 1 they pass the turn to the other player
  • If one gets another value other than 1 they can decide to continue playing or pass to the next player.
    For instance they decide to continue playing and get a 1 then all points collected during that play will be lost.
